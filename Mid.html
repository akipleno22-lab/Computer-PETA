Mercury

Small, rocky, and closest to the Sun. Extreme temperatures and a cratered landscape.

Venus

Thick atmosphere, scorching surface, and cloud-covered skies—Earth's hot sister.

Earth

Our home — a watery, life-bearing world with a protective atmosphere and diverse climates.

Mars

The red planet: a cold desert with towering volcanoes and a tantalizing history of water.

Jupiter

Giant gas planet with a stormy atmosphere and dozens of moons — the system's heavyweight.

Saturn

Famous for its rings — a gas giant with spectacular icy bands and many moons.

Uranus

An ice giant tipped on its side with faint rings and a chilly atmosphere of hydrogen and helium.

    Neptune

Neptune

Farthest classical planet — cold, windy, and deep blue with dynamic weather systems.
